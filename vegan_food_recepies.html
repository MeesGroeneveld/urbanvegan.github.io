Beetroot Burgers

ingridients

  • Pot gesneden bietjes
  • 2 red onions
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 egg
  • 100 g oatmeal
  • 1 large tomato
  • a hand full of basilicum
  • teaspoon of olife oil
  • brown buns
  • Dille saus
  • rucola as much as you see fit

preperation

Drain the beets and put them in the food processor, peel the onion and the garlic and add them. Add the basil and grind everything coarsely. Add the oatmeal, egg and a teaspoon of olive oil and mix briefly. Season with salt and pepper and let it sit for half an hour so that the oatmeal can absorb the liquid. Shape it into four patties. Do they fall apart? Then add some extra oatmeal. Fry or grill the burgers for 5 minutes on both sides. Wash and cut the tomato into slices and peel the remaining ones and cut them into slices as well. Top a sandwich with lettuce, tomato, onion and two beet burgers. Serve with dill sauce. Also delicious with a few slices of avocado.
